Beef Pho/Pho BÃ²

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds Beef Soup Bones
  • 1 package Thin Rice Noodes
  • 1 1/2 pound Beef top sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons Fish Sauce
  • 1 medium Onion unpeeled and cut in half
  • 5 slices fresh Ginger
  • 2 pods Star Anise
  • 1 tablespoon Salt
  • 4 quarts water
  • Garnish - Cilantro, Green Onion, Thai Basil, Lime, Hosion Sauce, Sriracha, and more.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degree F.
  2. Place beef bones on a baking sheet and roast in the preheated oven until browned. About 1 hour
  3. Place onions halves on a second baking sheet and roast on another rack until blackened and soft. About 45 minutes
  4. Transfer both Beef bone and Onions halves to a large stockpot. Add ginger, salt, star anise, fish sauce, 4 quarts water and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 6-10 hours, then strain broth into a sauce pan.
  6. Put rice noodles into a large bowl of room temperature water for 1 hour.
  7. Heat up broth by bringing it to a simmer and put a large pot of water to boil.
  8. Boil noodles for 1 minute and the drain.
  9. Put noodles into bowl along side sirloin, cilantro, green onions and ladle hot broth over the top.
  10. Stir and let the beef sit until it is cooked and no longer pink.
  11. Serve with bean sprouts, thai basil, lime wedge, hoisin sauce, chile-garlic, etc.
